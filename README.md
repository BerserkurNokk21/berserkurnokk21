### 👋 About Me

I'm a Spain-based developer working at the intersection of **game development** and **data engineering**. By day, I design and maintain ETL pipelines and PostgreSQL infrastructure for enterprise systems at **Winfra**; in my own time, I build games and tools in **Unity/C#**. I enjoy the contrast between crafting interactive player experiences and engineering reliable, scalable data flows — and I bring the same discipline for clean architecture to both worlds.
